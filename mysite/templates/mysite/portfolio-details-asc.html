A showcase of my Data Analysis expertise.

I led the comprehensive overhaul and digitization of the Ministry of Education's data collection processes, developing an advanced digital platform for school data collection, analysis, and reporting for the Annual School Census in 2019, 2020, and 2021. This landmark achievement, which had not been previously accomplished by the Ministry of Education in Liberia, was realized through my dedicated intervention and leadership. The initiative leveraged a suite of powerful tools: Pandas for sophisticated data manipulation, Matplotlib for detailed data visualization, LaTeX for professional document preparation, and KoboToolbox for seamless mobile data collection. This innovative system not only ensured accurate, efficient, and reliable data management but also set a new benchmark for educational data processes in Liberia. My work facilitated better decision-making, enhanced transparency, and contributed to the overall improvement of the educational infrastructure in the country.

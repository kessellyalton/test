A Glimpse into My Expertise in Education Planning and Assessment

Collaborating with experts from UNESCO, I spearheaded a comprehensive assessment of Liberia's entire education sector. This in-depth analysis provided critical insights into the strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities within the system. Utilizing the data obtained, I led a dedicated team in crafting a detailed five-year strategic plan aimed at transforming the sector. This ambitious plan addressed key challenges, set clear goals, and outlined actionable steps to enhance the quality and accessibility of education across Liberia. Our strategic approach included initiatives to improve teacher training, update curricula, increase student enrollment, and integrate technology into classrooms. Through this initiative, we established a robust framework for sustainable educational development and improvement, laying the groundwork for a brighter future for Liberia's educational landscape.
